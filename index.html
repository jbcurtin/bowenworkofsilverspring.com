After closing my office in October of 2020 due to COVID-19. I have decided to only continue my remote practice at this time.

Remote Bowenwork is a gentle method to balance out body energy. Using experience taken from ARCH and Reke Mastery, Katherine connects with her client’s energy over the phone. In the first session she’ll ask about your health history and current physical symptoms to determine the appropriate Bowen Protocol. She’ll then begin the healing session.

The healing session is unique for each client. She connects energetically with her clients to feel the client’s body responses. While connected, she visualizes physically doing movements on the body. Inbetween movements there are short waits as the body processes the method. After the healing session, the body will continue to integrate the work over the next three to five days.

Conditions that affect the musculoskeletal sheath as well as systemic issues are addressed.

  • Sprains
  • Strains
  • Overuse injuries
  • Sports injuries
  • Stress Reactions
  • Fatigue
  • Back pain
  • Headaches
  • Fibromyalgia
  • Digestion Issues
  • Ear/throat problems
  • Knee pain
